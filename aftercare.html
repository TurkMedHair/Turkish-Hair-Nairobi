Frequently Asked Questions

Complete Post-Op Protocol

ACTIVITY / REQUIREMENT TIMELINE REASON / GUIDELINE
Sleeping Position First 7 Days Sleep at a 45° angle (using 2 pillows) to prevent forehead swelling.
Direct Sunlight 14 Days UV rays can damage healing tissue and cause permanent pigmentation.
Smoking & Vaping 7 Days Pre/Post Nicotine constricts blood vessels, significantly lowering graft survival.
Alcohol Consumption 7 Days Post Alcohol thins the blood and interferes with the healing of incisions.
Sexual Activity 5 Days Prevents spikes in blood pressure that could dislodge new grafts.
Headwear / Caps 10 Days Strictly avoid friction. Only use the loose surgical hat provided.
Swimming (Salt/Chlorine) 30 Days Chemicals and bacteria in water can cause infection in donor sites.
Hair Dye / Barber Cut 6 Weeks Wait until the scalp is fully desensitized from chemical irritation.
Heavy Gym / Weightlifting 4 Weeks Prevents "stretch scars" in the donor area from physical strain.
Scratching / Picking Immediate Never pick scabs. Allow the Mossi Foam to soften them naturally.
