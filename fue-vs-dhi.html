FUE vs DHI: Which Technique is Right for You?

Choosing between **Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE)** and **Direct Hair Implantation (DHI)** is one of the most important decisions you will make for your hair restoration journey in Nairobi.

What is FUE?

In FUE, follicles are extracted individually and then channels are opened manually before implantation. It is ideal for covering large balding areas efficiently.

What is DHI?

DHI uses a specialized tool called the "Choi Pen." It allows for extraction and implantation to happen almost simultaneously without opening channels first. This is often preferred for increasing density in thinning areas without shaving the head.

Expert Recommendation

Our Turkish surgeons usually recommend DHI for beard transplants and female hairline lowering, while Sapphire FUE is king for maximum coverage.
