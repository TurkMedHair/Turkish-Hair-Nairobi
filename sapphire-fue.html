Precision Science

Why Sapphire?

Standard FUE uses steel blades which create wider, U-shaped incisions. Sapphire blades are made from a single crystal, allowing our surgeons to create **V-shaped** incisions.

  • 30% smaller entry points
  • Hyper-density (up to 80 grafts per cm²)
  • Near-zero scarring on the scalp

Real patient result: 4,200 Grafts

The 3-Day Recovery Timeline

Day 01: The Procedure

Under Dr. Ahmet's supervision, the channels are opened. Because of the sapphire precision, there is minimal bleeding and tissue trauma.

Day 02: Initial Wash

You return to our Valley View clinic for a medicated wash. The V-shaped incisions are already starting to close.

Day 03: Recovery Complete

Small scabs begin to fall off. Most patients return to office-based work today with full confidence.

Day 10: Permanent Bond

The follicles are now permanently rooted. You can resume light exercise and normal grooming habits.
