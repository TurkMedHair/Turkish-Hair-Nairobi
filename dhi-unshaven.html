Why Choi Pen DHI?

Traditional FUE requires "slits" to be made before planting. DHI skips this step. The follicle is loaded into the pen and injected directly, which means:

  • • Higher Survival: Follicles spend less time outside the body.
  • • No Shaving: We can navigate between your long hair.
  • • Faster Healing: Pin-point accuracy means the scalp heals in days, not weeks.
