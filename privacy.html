Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At TurkMed Nairobi, we are deeply committed to protecting the privacy of our patients. We collect personal data required exclusively to build optimized medical profiles, schedule precision evaluations, and arrange professional travel configurations where applicable.

  • Personal Identity Data (Full Name, Contact details, Date of Birth).
  • Digital Scalp Images uploaded during remote analysis requests.
  • Clinical History data pertinent directly to hair restoration eligibility.

2. How We Utilize Data

Your data is processed under absolute confidentiality directives. We utilize your records to:

  • Evaluate follicular density parameters and design personalized surgical plans.
  • Facilitate clinical consulting access between you and our certified experts.
  • Ensure seamless operational follow-ups throughout your recovery phases.

3. Secure Medical Data Preservation

All clinical images and patient data forms are stored using highly secure database architectures. We deploy strict industry-standard technical encryption matrices to completely isolate and eliminate unauthorized access profiles to your diagnostic imagery.

4. Your Rights & Erasure

Patients preserve the total right to inspect, correct, or request permanent structural removal of their stored profile histories from our communication ecosystems at any moment by contacting our Nairobi clinic administrative desk directly.
