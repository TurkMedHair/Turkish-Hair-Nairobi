Frequently Answered Medical Queries

While a standard hair transplant in Western countries ranges between $10,000 and $20,000, TurkMed Nairobi offers identical premium Turkish standards, including Sapphire FUE technology and Istanbul-trained master surgeons, right here in Nairobi for a highly competitive investment. This eliminates the massive financial overhead of long-haul international flights, language barriers, and distant unmonitored post-operative care, saving patients thousands of dollars while yielding superior results.

Standard FUE utilizes traditional micro-blades made from stainless steel to slice initial recipient channels. In contrast, Sapphire FUE uses ultra-sharp blades ground from single-crystal genuine sapphires. These sapphire crystals leave micro-incisions that are up to 30% smaller, substantially minimizing tissue bleeding, accelerating recovery timelines, preventing post-op scabbing, and allowing follicular micro-grafts to be implanted infinitely closer to match natural hair density layout perfectly.

The initial superficial healing cycle concludes within 7 to 10 days post-procedure, by which time scabs dissolve completely via controlled washings. From weeks 3 to 6, implanted hairs undergo a natural physiological shedding period called "shock loss." The robust follicular root complexes remain secure under the dermis. New visible permanent hair shafts begin pushing through around Month 3 to 4, reaching 60% density at Month 6, and solidifying to definitive mature structural thickness by Month 12.

The entire operation is categorized as near-painless. Prior to any follicular extraction phase or micro sapphire canal design sequence, we deploy localized micro-needle anesthetic chemical compounds. This thoroughly numbs the target nerve pathways of the dermal skin layers. After the initial 5-minute numbing segment takes effect, patients feel absolute zero discomfort and can read, stream media, or rest calmly during the 5 to 7 hour operational cycle.
