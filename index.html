Clinical FAQ

Why choose Dr. Ahmet Başkaya for Afro hair?

Afro-hair follicles are curved under the skin. Dr. Ahmet uses specialized Turkish curved punches that follow the follicle's natural shape, preventing transection (damage) and ensuring 100% growth.

Is the Sapphire FUE technique painful?

We use a localized "Comfort-In" anesthesia system. You will feel no pain during the 6-8 hour procedure and can relax, watch movies, or listen to music.

How long until I see the final result?

Growth begins at month 3. By month 6, you will see significant coverage. The full, final density is achieved between months 12 and 14.

What is the aftercare protocol?

Dr. Ahmet's team provides a full 1-year aftercare kit, including specialized Turkish shampoos, biotin supplements, and monthly PRP sessions in our Nairobi clinic.
