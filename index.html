The Medical Expert FAQ

Everything you need to know before your procedure.

How long does the procedure take? +
Usually 6 to 8 hours depending on the number of grafts. You will have breaks for lunch and rest.
When can I return to work? +
Most patients return to office work within 3-5 days. If your job involves heavy lifting, we recommend 14 days.
Is the result permanent? +
Yes. Hair from the donor zone (back and sides) is genetically resistant to DHT, the hormone that causes balding.
Does it work for women? +
Absolutely. We use the DHI technique for women which allows us to restore density without shaving the head.
What is the difference between standard FUE and Sapphire FUE? +
Standard FUE uses steel micro-blades to open micro-channels. Sapphire FUE utilizes custom single-crystal V-shaped sapphire gemstone edges which dramatically reduce cellular trauma, bleeding, and recovery times down to just 72 hours.
How is the design of Afro-textured curly hairlines custom managed? +
Afro-textured hair curves significantly underneath the dermis line. Our specialized, patented ultra-thin curved punches track the subterranean path of the root safely without creating transections, ensuring 100% vital preservation.
Are there structural finance plans accessible in Nairobi? +
Yes, we provide flexible, customizable localized payment pathways and strategic banking institution payment partnership options to allow structured operational ease across months.
