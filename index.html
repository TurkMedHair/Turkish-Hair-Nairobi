What is Hair Transplant?

A hair transplant is a medically proven, permanent hair restoration procedure that involves relocating healthy hair follicles from a donor area to areas affected by hair loss or thinning.

Uses Your Own Hair

Hair follicles are taken from areas resistant to loss and implanted into thinning regions, ensuring natural growth with no rejection.

Advanced Medical Technique

Modern methods such as FUE allow precise placement of follicles for natural density and realistic hairlines.

Natural & Permanent Results

Once transplanted, the hair continues to grow like normal hair—you can wash it, cut it, and style it naturally for life.

Safe & Clinically Proven

Hair transplantation is a safe, minimally invasive procedure performed by experienced specialists under international standards.

Boosts Confidence

A transplant restores confidence, youthfulness, and self-esteem—positively impacting personal and professional life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about hair restoration at TurkMed Nairobi.

Does it work on African hair?

Yes. African hair follicles are curved, which requires specialized extraction techniques. Our Turkish team is specifically trained in high-density FUE for Afro-textured hair to prevent follicle damage.

How long does the procedure take?

A typical session takes between 6 to 8 hours. You can relax, watch a movie, or use your phone during the procedure. You go home the same day.

Is the result permanent?

Yes. We transplant hair from the "safe zone" at the back of the head. These follicles are genetically resistant to DHT (the hormone that causes balding), so they will grow for the rest of your life.

What is the cost of hair transplant in Nairobi?

Pricing depends on the number of grafts needed. We provide transparent, all-inclusive packages that cover the surgery, medical kit, and PRP therapy. Contact us for a custom quote.
