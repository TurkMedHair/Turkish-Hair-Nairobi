Medical Intelligence FAQ

Clear, direct answers regarding recovery, surgical mechanics, and growth expectations.

How long does a typical hair transplant session require? +
Most operations require 6 to 8 hours depending upon the aggregate graft count planned. The session is structured with custom catered meal breaks inside private recovery rooms.
What is the baseline recovery timeline before returning to work? +
Most patients return to desktop or office-bound employment tracks within 3 to 5 days post-procedure. For strenuous labor or heavy gym training cycles, we strictly advise a 14-day clearance.
Are the final hair density adjustments fully permanent? +
Yes. Hair units collected from the secure posterior occipital zone are genetically insulated against DHT hormones, ensuring lifetime cellular survival and growth cycles.
Can female hairline thinning be successfully treated? +
Absolutely. Our advanced DHI Choi implanter systems bypass total head shaving mandates, allowing us to drop dense grafts directly between existing long hair fibers safely.
