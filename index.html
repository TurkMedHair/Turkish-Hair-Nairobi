About TurkMed Nairobi

TurkMed is a leading hair restoration clinic bringing world-class Turkish medical expertise to Kenya. Our team specializes in providing natural, high-density hair transplants specifically tailored for African hair textures.

We believe that everyone deserves to feel confident. By combining the latest FUE and DHI technology with affordable local pricing, we eliminate the need for expensive international travel.

Our Specialized Services

FUE Hair Transplant

The gold standard in hair restoration. Individual follicles are extracted and transplanted with precision, leaving no linear scars and ensuring a fast recovery.

DHI Technique

Direct Hair Implantation allows for maximum density and control over the angle of hair growth, providing the most natural-looking results possible.

Beard & Eyebrow

We use specialized micro-grafting techniques to restore thickness and shape to facial hair, following the natural contours of your face.
