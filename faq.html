The Procedures

Sapphire FUE utilizes precision blades crafted from genuine micro-sapphire crystal to create individual recipient channels prior to graft insertion, allowing for high density. DHI (Direct Hair Implantation) uses a specialized implanter pen tool to opening channels and seat the graft simultaneously, allowing precision vector matching and an option for completely unshaven treatments.

The procedure is executed under localized advanced numbing matrices. Patients experience minimal discomfort during the initial administration phase; after anesthesia takes effect, the extraction and implantation cycles are completely painless.

Recovery & Results

Most patients return to light desk-based workflow within 2 to 3 days post-operation. Micro-crusting cleans away seamlessly inside 10 days during your customized clinical washing cycle protocols. Heavy physical exercise or direct weight lifting should be suspended for a full 20 to 30 days to protect newly integrated grafts.

Initial shedding phases occur normally within weeks 2 to 6. Transplanted follicles initiate structural growth cycles around month 3. Noticeable stylistic density improvements appear standard from months 5 to 6, with ultimate crown density stabilization finalized around month 12 to 14.
