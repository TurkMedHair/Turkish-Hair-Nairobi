5 Essential Tips for Faster Recovery After Your Transplant

Expert post-operative care advice from the TurkMed Nairobi team.

The success of a hair transplant doesn't end when you leave the clinic. The first 14 days are the "golden window" for graft survival. At TurkMed Nairobi, we ensure you have the tools, but your daily care at home makes the difference.

1. The 45-Degree Sleep Rule

For the first 3 to 5 nights, sleep with your head elevated using two or three pillows. This prevents forehead swelling and ensures your new grafts don't rub against the bedding.

2. Hands Off the Grafts

It is tempting to touch the tiny crusts that form, but you must resist! These scabs are anchoring your new follicles. Touching them can dislodge the graft before it has taken root.

3. Specialized Washing Technique

After 48 hours, you will begin washing your hair using the specialized foam and shampoo provided in your TurkMed aftercare kit. Use lukewarm water and a very gentle "tapping" motion rather than scrubbing.

4. Avoid Intense Activity

Blood pressure plays a huge role in healing. Avoid the gym, heavy lifting, or strenuous exercise for at least 14 days to prevent the grafts from "popping" out due to increased pressure.

5. Shield from the Sun

The scalp is very sensitive after surgery. Avoid direct sunlight for the first month. If you must go outside, we will provide a specific loose-fitting hat that won't compress the transplanted area.
