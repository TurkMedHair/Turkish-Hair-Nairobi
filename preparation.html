Protocol for Success

Your Preparation

The 7-Day Countdown

1. Medical History

Inform our clinic of any blood thinners or chronic medications. We require a blood test (done on-site) to ensure optimal healing conditions.

2. Lifestyle Adjustments

Stop smoking and alcohol 7 days before. Nicotine constricts blood flow, which can starve the new grafts of oxygen.

3. The Night Before

Wash your hair with a neutral shampoo. Do not apply any gels or oils. Get at least 8 hours of sleep for a relaxed procedure day.

The Day of Surgery

  • Dress Code: Wear a button-down shirt. You must not pull anything over your head after the surgery.
  • Breakfast: Eat a light, healthy meal. Avoid excessive caffeine as it can increase blood pressure.
